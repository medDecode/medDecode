Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

medDecode is committed to protecting the privacy of every user. We collect only the information necessary to provide accurate interpretation of your medical test results. All uploaded data are stored securely and used exclusively to generate personalized reports.

Information We Collect
  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, phone number)
  • Medical test results and health indicators
  • Usage data and analytics
  • Communication preferences
How We Use Your Information

Users have full control over their personal information and can request its deletion or correction at any time. No data are shared with third parties without explicit consent, except as required by law. Our system complies with international standards of personal data protection, including GDPR.

Your Rights: You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time through your account settings or by contacting our support team.

Data Security

Last updated: December 2024

We take data security extremely seriously. All communications between your device and our servers are encrypted using SSL technology, ensuring that your data cannot be intercepted or tampered with.

Security Measures
End-to-End Encryption
Secure Data Centers
Multi-Factor Authentication
Regular Backups

medDecode stores information on protected servers located in secure data centers, with regular security audits and multi-level access control. Internal data handling procedures follow strict protocols to prevent unauthorized access, leaks, or data loss.

Additionally, backup copies of data are created automatically to ensure the stability and reliability of the service in the event of technical failure.

Cookies Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Our website uses cookies to improve functionality, analyze usage statistics, and personalize user experience. Cookies allow us to remember your preferences, such as selected language or display settings, for a smoother browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use
Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Basic website functionality Session
Preference Cookies Remember your settings 1 Year
Analytics Cookies Website improvement 2 Years

We do not use cookies to collect sensitive personal data. You can modify your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of the website but will not restrict access to main functionalities.

Accessibility Statement

Last updated: December 2024

medDecode is designed to be accessible to all users, including people with disabilities. We strive to maintain compliance with WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) standards and continuously improve our interface to ensure an inclusive experience.

Accessibility Features
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • High contrast mode
  • Text resizing capabilities
  • Alternative text for images
  • Clear and consistent layout

Text readability, contrast, navigation structure, and alternative text for images are optimized to make the platform as user-friendly as possible. We welcome feedback from users to further enhance accessibility and usability across different devices and assistive technologies.

Need assistance? If you encounter any accessibility barriers, please contact our support team.

Service Limitations

Last updated: December 2024

Important Notice: The information provided by medDecode is for educational and informational purposes only.

Our system analyzes test results automatically and provides general health insights, but it cannot replace professional medical diagnosis or treatment.

What Our Service Provides
  • Automated analysis of medical test results
  • Comparison with standard reference ranges
  • Educational information about health indicators
  • Recommendations for specialist consultations
What Our Service Does Not Provide
  • Medical diagnosis or treatment plans
  • Emergency medical advice
  • Prescription recommendations
  • Replacement for doctor's consultation

Users are encouraged to consult certified medical specialists for an in-depth evaluation of their condition. medDecode assumes no responsibility for decisions made based solely on automated analysis results. The platform is intended to support, not substitute, the expertise of healthcare professionals.
